Waffles

Description

These classic homemade waffles are the perfect breakfast treat — golden and crispy on the outside while staying light, fluffy, and tender on the inside. With a subtle sweetness and rich buttery flavor, they're incredibly versatile: top them with maple syrup, fresh fruit, whipped cream, or even go savoury with fried chicken. This easy recipe uses simple pantry method and comes together quickly, making it ideal for lazy weekend mornings or a special brunch in Town.

Ingredients (makes about 5–6 standard waffles):

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1½ cups milk (warm if possible for extra fluffiness)li>
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted (or 6 tablespoons)
  • 1sp tea spoon vanilla extract (optional, for extra flavor)

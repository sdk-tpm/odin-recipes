stew

Description

This meaty beef stew is the ultimate comfort food, perfect for chilly evenings or whenever you crave something warm and satisfying. Tender chunks of beef are slowly simmered until melt-in-your-mouth tender, mingling with classic vegetables like carrots, potatoes, onions, and celery in a rich, savory broth deepened with herbs, a touch of red wine or tomato for complexity, and layers of flavor from slow cooking. The result is a thick, comforting one-pot meal where every spoonful delivers deep, beefy goodness and perfectly cooked veggies that soak up the aromatic sauceâ€”pure soul-warming deliciousness that's even better the next day.

Whether served with crusty bread for dipping or over rice, this timeless stew brings cozy, home-cooked nostalgia to the table and makes any gathering feel extra special.

  • 600g Beef
  • 6 Carrots
  • 3 Potatoes
